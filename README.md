# Car Sales Analysis Dashboard Power-BI

## 📌 Project Overview
Interactive Car Sales Analysis Dashboard developed using Power BI. It provides insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, geographic sales distribution, and deal size analysis to support data-driven business decisions.

## 🎯 Business Objective

To analyze car sales data and identify revenue trends, customer segments, product performance, regional sales opportunities, and order distribution through interactive dashboards.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Total Sales reached **9.76M** across **298 orders**.
- Medium-sized deals generated the highest revenue.
- Classic Cars is the top-performing product line.
- Shipped orders account for the vast majority of fulfilled orders.
- USA is the highest revenue-generating country.
- Madrid is the top-performing city.
- Customer purchases are primarily concentrated in Medium and Small deal sizes.

---
## 💡 Recommendations

- Increase inventory for Classic Cars due to consistently high demand.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing countries such as the USA, Spain, and France.
- Strengthen customer loyalty programs for top-value customers.
- Improve conversion of Large Deal Size opportunities through targeted sales strategies.
- Investigate cancelled and disputed orders to improve fulfillment performance.
- Expand sales efforts in emerging markets with lower revenue contribution.
